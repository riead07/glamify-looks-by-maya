About Our Salon

Glamify Looks by Maya

Welcome to Glamify Looks by Maya, a premier beauty studio located in Agargaon, Dhaka. Our salon is founded on the belief that beauty lies in details, and every client deserves personalized, high-quality care.

From professional bridal makeovers that turn heads on your special day, to high-end L'Oréal professional hair treatments that restore damaged locks, we are committed to using only top-tier products and techniques. Whether it is a haircut, keratin smooth, premium facial, or elegant manicure, our goal is to deliver an unforgettable luxury experience.
